About us

Green Park has been an activity and vacation spot since 2008. The proximity to Nairobi, makes it a saught after location for families and solo travellers. The local surroundings are Lake Naivasha where our guests go out fishing and on water safaris to spot aquatic game. We also provide safari services in nearby parks. Closely located we have ranges of mountains making for tough and rewarding hikes with beautiful views. Don't wait book your stay at Green Park today!
