Activities

Green Park has a wide range of activities that you can easily book and enjoy. We make it easy for you to find the activities you want to do. Through our expert tour guides and local experts we offer you a unique opportunity to experience Kenya. The surrounding mountain ranges offer you a unmatched view of the surrounding areas. The game parks give you the opportunity to see big game in their natural habitat. Lake Naivasha offers you the chance to try fresh local fish and embark on water safaris! We organize everything, so get ready to sit back and relax at Green Park!
